02 THE STORY

A Legacy of Excellence

Born from a passion for preserving India's vegetarian culinary heritage, UTTARAYAN brings together flavors from every corner of the subcontinent.

Our name derives from the sacred festival of Uttarayan, celebrating the sun's northward journey — a symbol of enlightenment and abundance.

Each dish tells a story, crafted with spices sourced directly from the farms of Rajasthan, the coconut groves of Kerala, and the wheat fields of Punjab.

For over two decades, we have welcomed guests into our warm embrace, offering not just a meal, but an unforgettable journey through India's vegetarian legacy.
